About the company

Obsessive about the details
you're never meant to notice.

Harts Generation started with a simple belief: great sound should disappear. When it's right, the audience forgets we're there — they just feel the moment. We bring over a decade of front-of-house, monitors and RF experience to every gig, whether it's a stadium headline slot or an intimate acoustic set.

We treat every event like it's being recorded for release — because increasingly, it is. Meticulous gain structure, clean RF coordination, and calm under pressure are non-negotiable.
